KConnect

where knowledge connects people

Download .zip Download .tar.gz View on GitHub

KConnect is an academic project.

This PHP application is a public forum which helps the students to post their questions and in response they can share their ideas by commenting answers to those questions. This will not only connect the people on the grounds of knowledge, but also creates a healthy discussion virtually.
